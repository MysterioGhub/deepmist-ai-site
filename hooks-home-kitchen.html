What makes a kitchen hook work

Show the mess first

Viewers stop for the kitchen they recognize — the cluttered counter, the dull knife, the overflowing drawer. Lead with that, not your product.

One visible transformation

The whole hook is built around one before-and-after you can see in a single shot. If the change isn't visible, the hook dies.

Name a real cost

Time, money, or daily annoyance. A hook lands harder when it puts a number on what the problem is already costing them.

Use these honestly

These are scripted ad openers — the same way any commercial is scripted. They are written to be adapted to something true about your product, not to invent something that isn't.

Keep them clean: don't bolt on fabricated numbers, invented review counts, fake scarcity, or results you can't stand behind. A hook earns the next three seconds. Your product has to earn the rest.
