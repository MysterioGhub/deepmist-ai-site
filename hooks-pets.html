What works in pets

Real beats staged

Pet owners spot a forced reaction instantly. The hook only lands if the footage shows the animal doing something genuine, like leaning in or refusing the toy.

Lead with the before

Pulling on the leash, shedding everywhere, meltdowns at the door. State the familiar problem first, then the fix, and pet parents stay to watch.

One pet, one habit

Specific beats general. 'My dog stopped pulling in two walks' reads as stronger than 'train your dog faster' because the claim is narrow and easy to show.

Use these honestly

These are scripted ad openers — the same way any commercial is scripted. They are written to be adapted to something true about your product, not to invent something that isn't.

Keep them clean: don't bolt on fabricated numbers, invented review counts, fake scarcity, or results you can't stand behind. A hook earns the next three seconds. Your product has to earn the rest.
