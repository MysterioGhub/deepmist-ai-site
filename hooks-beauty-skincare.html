What works in skincare

Name the feeling

Buyers do not search for ingredients, they search for a feeling: tired-looking, tight, dull, patchy. Open on the feeling and the product follows.

One step, not a routine

A twelve-step routine is a commitment. A hook that isolates a single missing step is an easier thing for a stranger to say yes to.

Understate the result

This category is saturated with overclaiming, so restraint reads as credible. Never bolt an invented statistic onto a hook to strengthen it.

Use these honestly

These are scripted ad openers — the same way any commercial is scripted. They are written to be adapted to something true about your product, not to invent something that isn't.

Keep them clean: don't bolt on fabricated numbers, invented review counts, fake scarcity, or results you can't stand behind. A hook earns the next three seconds. Your product has to earn the rest.
