30 hooks that stop the scroll.

The first three seconds decide whether your ad gets watched or swiped past. These are 30 opening lines from the hook bank we write our own video ads against — sorted by niche, free to take and adapt. No email, no download gate.

How to use one

  1. Pick the line closest to your product's real benefit.
  2. Swap in your specifics — the product, the timeframe, the actual result.
  3. Say it in the first 3 seconds, on camera or voiceover, while the product is already visible.
  4. Cut the setup. If the hook isn't the first thing heard, it isn't a hook.

Use these honestly

These are scripted ad openers — the same way any commercial is scripted. They are written to be adapted to something true about your product, not to invent something that isn't.

Keep them clean: don't bolt on fabricated numbers, invented review counts, fake scarcity, or results you can't stand behind. A hook earns the next three seconds. Your product has to earn the rest.
