What works in fragrance

Borrow a sense

The camera cannot smell. Reach for what it can show instead: a room going quiet, a head turning, a scarf lifted at a gathering.

Longevity is the argument

Most fragrance complaints are about a scent fading. A hook built on how long it lasts speaks to the objection buyers already have.

Respect the buyer

Gulf fragrance buyers know oud. Vague luxury language reads as bluff. Specifics about origin, layering or wear time earn attention.

Use these honestly

These are scripted ad openers — the same way any commercial is scripted. They are written to be adapted to something true about your product, not to invent something that isn't.

Keep them clean: don't bolt on fabricated numbers, invented review counts, fake scarcity, or results you can't stand behind. A hook earns the next three seconds. Your product has to earn the rest.
